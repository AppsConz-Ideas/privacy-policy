Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2026

Screenshot Organizer is a simple offline app designed to help users organize and manage their screenshots.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app does not require:

  • Login or signup
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data

Storage Access

The app only uses storage permission to access screenshots stored on your device.

This permission is used solely for:

  • Organizing screenshots into categories
  • Viewing and managing screenshots
  • Adding favorites
  • Deleting unwanted screenshots

All data remains on your device. No screenshots or information are uploaded to any server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Screenshot Organizer does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
