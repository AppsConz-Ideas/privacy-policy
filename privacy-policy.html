Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Habit Tracker mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

The Habit Tracker app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

We do not require user registration, login, or account creation.

Local Data Storage

All data entered into the app (such as habits, progress, or settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

No Internet-Based Data Collection

The app does not send any personal or usage data to external servers. We do not use analytics tools, tracking systems, or advertising identifiers.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on the user’s device, data security depends on the security of the device itself.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children or any users.
